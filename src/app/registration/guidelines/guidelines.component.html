Guidelines

Visitors are required to reserve a day pass at participating parks.

1

Arrive Prepared

Please arrive with your trip planned and your pass downloaded or printed. In order to make the most of your pass, ensure you arrive with ample time to complete your intended activities.

2

Cancelations

Only reserve passes that you intend to use. If you are no longer in need of a previously reserved pass, please cancel according by following the instructions provided in your confirmation email.
