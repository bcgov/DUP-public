Guidelines

As of June 22, 2021, visitors are required to reserve a day pass to visit participating parks. Depending on the park, a day-use pass may not be required for all trails. If the park is open and the trail or trailhead isn’t listed in the menu below, you do not need to reserve a day pass. Check bcparks.ca for more details.

1

Cancellations

Please cancel according to the instructions in your confirmation email if the passes you’ve reserved are no longer needed.

2

Space

Passes do not guarantee a parking spot – visitors should plan accordingly.

3

Preparation

Preparation Always travel within your ability and prepare for your trip.

4

In-Park

Follow in-park signage and have your pass available while in the park and on the trail.
