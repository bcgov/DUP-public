Reserve a day-use pass

Day-use passes are required at some popular BC Parks during the summer and winter seasons. Reserving a pass helps us manage the number of park visitors, so we can protect the park and you can have a better visiting experience.

There is no available first come, first served parking, so book ahead to avoid disappointment.

Day-use pass
  • Some parks require vehicle passes. These are valid for up to 12 people (adults and children) travelling in the same vehicle.
  • Other parks require trail passes. These must be booked for each individual person, per day. A maximum of 4 passes may be booked per reservation. Children 12 and under accompanied by an adult do not require a pass.
