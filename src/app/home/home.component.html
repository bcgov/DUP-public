Reserve a Day Use Pass

The BC Parks Day Use Pilot Project is continuing for the 2021 winter season to help manage visitation levels at Mount Seymour Provincial Park, one of British Columbia's most popular winter day use parks.

Visitors who intend on using the backcountry trail network at Mount Seymour Park are now required to reserve an a.m. or p.m. day use parking pass for their visit, as there will be no available first come, first serve parking during the day (7:00am–4:00pm) from December 15, 2021 to March 31, 2022.

Day Use Vehicle Pass

This pass is not intended for those who have resort passes. As seen on the parking map , Mount Seymour resort has additional parking spaces intended solely for individuals using resort facilities. Please do not reserve a pass if you are using resort facilities.
