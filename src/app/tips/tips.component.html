Keys to Visiting the Great Outdoors

1

Trip plan

Check bcparks.ca for updates. Know what the weather and conditions will be. Download and use AdventureSmart's Trip Plan app to share your plan with a trusted contact.

2

Train

Obtain knowledge and skills you need before heading out. Know and stay within your limits.

3

Take the essentials

Pack the basic survival items you should have in any outdoor situation

4

Responsible Recreation

Parks aren't self cleaning. Leave natural and cultural objects undisturbed. Keep pets on leash. Nice people make nature nicer. Learn more bcparks.ca/beresponsible.

5

Keep wildlife wild

Wild animals are supposed to eat wild things. Please give wildlife space and store your food properly.

6

Enjoy your visit

Enjoy your visit For more information on the day pass pilot program, including what to expect in park, please visit us at bcparks.ca/reserve/day-use .
