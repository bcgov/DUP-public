Keys to Visiting the Great Outdoors

1

Plan Ahead

Prepare for your trip. Travel within your abilities and check weather conditions before your trip. Have your park pass loaded before you arrive to the park, as mobile internet connectivity can be unreliable. Share your plan with a trusted contact.

2

Use Available Resources

Check the BC Parks home page for important updates and notices. Download and use AdventureSmart's Trip Plan app.

3

Pack the Essentials

Nature is unpredictable; do your own research and pack the essentials — be prepared for anything.

4

Recreate Responsibly

Parks are not self-cleaning — pack out all garbage, recyclables, and organics. Organic matter can attract wildlife. Learn more here.

5

Keep Wildlife Wild

Parks are home to all kinds of wildlife. Follow in-park signage to respect their natural habitat and limit encounters. Please refrain from feeding any wild animals.

6

Enjoy

Enjoy your visit. For more information on the day pass pilot program, including what to expect in park, please visit us here.
